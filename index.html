Weasyl Projects

Weasyl Projects is a place for code that wouldn't necessarily belong on the Weasyl mainsite. Some projects are third-party code developed specifically for Weasyl, are not official Weasyl products, and do not represent Weasyl views and opinions.

  • Simple Ottermatic Follower Transfer, a migration assistant for Weasyl users coming from Fur Affinity, by Dr. Dos
  • Conbadger, a tool for generating pretty QR code badges that link to Weasyl profiles, by simonfox and weykent.
  • Art Gallery Manager:
    Enables artists to manage their galleries offline and batch-upload submissions to multiple websites. Also enables consumers of art to manage galleries of downloaded art.
    by Kitashi.
  • Waxpost, a Chrome extension that simplifies crossposting from other art sites to Weasyl, by weykent.
  • A user style for showing the title and author below image thumbnails instead of on rollover, by Simon.
